02 — Production Logistics

How We Built It

Every production decision is documented below — from pre-production planning through final delivery.

🎯
Phase 01
Discovery & Brief
Client intake, creative brief development, objective mapping, audience profiling, and platform strategy alignment. Duration: Days 1–2.
📋
Phase 02
Pre-Production
Script writing, storyboarding, shot list, location/talent coordination, asset sourcing, and technical spec lock. Duration: Days 3–5.
🎬
Phase 03
Production
Principal photography, drone capture, b-roll, voiceover recording, and on-set AI-assisted direction support. Duration: Days 6–8.
✂️
Phase 04
Post-Production
Offline edit, color grade, sound design, AI-enhanced VFX, motion graphics, and subtitle/caption generation. Duration: Days 9–12.
🔍
Phase 05
Review & Revisions
Two structured revision rounds with timestamped feedback workflow. Client portal delivery + approval sign-off. Duration: Days 13–14.
🚀
Phase 06
Delivery & Publish
Platform-optimized exports, upload, metadata tagging, ad account asset push, and 30-day post-launch monitoring. Duration: Day 14+.
