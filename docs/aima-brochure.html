Who
We Are.
"We are storytellers first. Every project begins with a human narrative — your story, your brand's mission, your audience's deepest desire. Then we deploy the most advanced AI production stack in the world to bring that story to life."

AIMA was built on one uncompromising conviction: generative AI is not a shortcut — it is a new creative instrument. Like electricity transformed music production in the 1950s, and digital technology reimagined cinema in the 1990s, AI is rewriting the laws of what's possible in creative production.

Founded in San Francisco by a team of martial artists, traders, producers, and technologists, AIMA sits at the intersection of financial intelligence, creative excellence, and technological innovation.

Our deeper mission: make world-class creative production accessible to every visionary — and build a legacy of generational wealth and philanthropic impact through the art of storytelling.

Chapter 07
The Production
Pipeline.

Six transparent steps from first conversation to final delivery. You always know where your project stands. No black boxes. No vague timelines.

01

Discovery & Creative Brief

30-minute intake call or detailed online form. We document your story, audience, goals, and visual references into a locked Creative Brief — approved by you before a single frame is generated. No production begins without full alignment.

Notion · Calendly · Miro · Loom
02

Concept & Storyboard

Within 24 hours of brief approval, our team delivers 2–3 AI-rendered concept directions — each with visual storyboard frames, a narrative treatment, and a color moodboard. You choose the direction and we lock scope.

MidJourney v7 · DALL·E 3 · Kling · Figma
03

AI Production

Video generation, avatar animation, voice synthesis, music composition, motion graphics, and sound design run simultaneously. Most projects produce a first draft within 24–72 hours of concept approval. Traditional studios need weeks for what we deliver in days.

Kling · Runway · ElevenLabs · Suno · HeyGen
04

Post-Production & Polish

Human-guided final assembly: color grading, audio mastering, subtitle work, format optimization, and quality assurance against the locked Creative Brief. Our creative director reviews every frame before it reaches your inbox.

CapCut Pro · Logic Pro · DaVinci · After Effects
05

Review & Revisions

Private password-protected review link with frame-level timestamped feedback. Leave notes directly on the video. Revisions returned within 24 hours per round. Additional rounds available at $200 each.

Frame.io · Loom · Slack · Notion Revision Log
06

Delivery & Automation

All files delivered in every required format via branded shared drive. Sovereign-tier clients receive a custom n8n automation pipeline for ongoing publishing and analytics — documented and handed off with training.

Google Drive · n8n · Zapier · Notion Handoff

Common Questions
Will AI video look as good as traditionally shot content?

At our quality tier — Kling AI, Runway Gen-3, 4K with professional color grading — the output is indistinguishable from traditionally produced content in most contexts. Our case studies include broadcast TV placements and 280K-view music videos.

Do I own the rights to the content?

Yes — full commercial license transfer is included with every project at no additional charge. You own the final delivered files outright, in perpetuity, for use across any platform.

Can you match my existing brand's visual style?

Absolutely. We conduct a full brand audit — collecting your existing assets, color system, typography, and visual references — and build AI prompting frameworks calibrated specifically to your brand identity.

What if I'm not satisfied?

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If after all included revision rounds the deliverable doesn't meet the locked Creative Brief, we will reshoot or refund — your choice. We've never had to issue a refund.

Can you handle multiple languages?

Yes — ElevenLabs supports 29+ languages with voice cloning. We've produced content in English, Spanish, French, Tagalog, Mandarin, Arabic, and Portuguese. Localization is +$300 per additional language.

Do you offer white-label for agencies?

Yes — dedicated white-label program for agencies and PR firms. Volume pricing, NDA agreements, and dedicated account management. Email agencies@aimediaagency.com to discuss partnership rates.

A multidisciplinary practitioner with roots in martial arts, financial markets, and digital production, Joselito brings a rare combination of strategic rigor and creative intuition to every engagement. His work sits at the intersection of financial intelligence, technological innovation, and human storytelling — informed by decades of experience across trading, content production, and systems design.

Under his direction, AIMA has delivered cinema-grade music videos, broadcast TV commercials, corporate explainer campaigns, and full-stack AI automation pipelines — all produced with generative AI tools at a fraction of traditional studio costs. His portfolio includes projects that have aired on broadcast television, accumulated hundreds of thousands of views, and earned recognition for their production quality.

Beyond the studio, Joselito is building toward a deeper purpose: generational wealth and philanthropic impact through the art of storytelling.
