AIMA was built on one uncompromising conviction: generative AI is not a shortcut — it is a new creative instrument. Our clients are artists, entrepreneurs, educators, health practitioners, and enterprise brands who refuse to accept the old equation: quality costs time, and time costs money. FAQ

Common Questions

Will AI-generated video look as good as traditionally shot content? ⌄
At the quality tier we operate at — Kling AI, Runway Gen-3, 4K output with professional color grading — the output is indistinguishable from traditionally produced content in most contexts. Our case studies include broadcast TV placements and 200K+ view music videos. The question isn't whether AI can match quality; it's whether your audience can tell the difference. They can't.
Do I own the rights to the content produced? ⌄
Yes — full commercial license transfer is included with every project at no additional charge. You own the final delivered files outright and may use them across any platform, in perpetuity, without royalty. Source files (PSD, AEP, stems) are available as an add-on for $400.
How does voice cloning work, and is it legal? ⌄
Voice cloning is performed exclusively with the consent of the voice owner — either your own voice, a contracted voice actor who has approved cloning, or a licensed synthetic voice. We use ElevenLabs' commercial API, which requires explicit consent workflows. We will never clone a voice without proper authorization.
Can you match my existing brand's visual style? ⌄
Absolutely. During the Discovery phase, we conduct a brand audit — collecting your existing assets, color system, typography, tone-of-voice guidelines, and visual references. We build AI prompting frameworks specifically calibrated to your brand identity, ensuring consistency across every deliverable.
What if I'm not satisfied with the final result? ⌄
We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If after all included revision rounds the deliverable does not meet the locked Creative Brief, we will reshoot or refund your project in full — whichever you prefer. In 3 years of operation, we have never issued a refund because we do not start production without a fully aligned brief.
Can you handle multi-language content? ⌄
Yes — ElevenLabs supports 29+ languages with voice cloning capability, and we've produced content in English, Spanish, French, Tagalog, Mandarin, Arabic, and Portuguese. Language localization is available as an add-on at $300 per additional language, which includes a re-voiced audio track and localized subtitle file.
How do payments work? ⌄
We require a 50% deposit to begin production, with the remaining balance due upon final delivery. For Sovereign and enterprise clients, we offer milestone-based payment schedules. We accept ACH, wire transfer, credit card (3% processing fee), and cryptocurrency (USDC preferred). All payments secured via Stripe or direct invoice.
Do you offer white-label services for agencies? ⌄
Yes — we have a dedicated white-label program for marketing agencies, PR firms, and media buyers who want to offer AI production services under their own brand. Volume pricing, NDA agreements, and dedicated account management are all available. Contact us to discuss agency partnership rates.
