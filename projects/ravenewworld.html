02 — Recording Logistics

How We Recorded It

Every recording decision documented — from songwriting to final master.

🎵
Phase 01
Songwriting & Pre-Production
Lyrics, chord progressions, demos, arrangement, BPM lock, key signature, vocal range mapping. Duration: Days 1–3.
🎹
Phase 02
Instrumental Tracking
Drums, bass, guitar, keys, synths, MIDI programming, AI-assisted orchestration, stems export. Duration: Days 4–8.
🎤
Phase 03
Vocal Recording
Lead vocals, harmonies, ad-libs, vocal comping, pitch correction, breath control, ElevenLabs voice cloning (if applicable). Duration: Days 9–12.
🎚️
Phase 04
Mixing
EQ, compression, reverb, delay, automation, stereo imaging, AI-assisted mixing (iZotope Neutron). Duration: Days 13–16.
💿
Phase 05
Mastering
Final EQ, limiting, loudness standards (LUFS), platform optimization (Spotify/Apple/YouTube), AI mastering (iZotope Ozone). Duration: Days 17–19.
🚀
Phase 06
Distribution & Publishing
Platform uploads (Spotify, Apple, YouTube Music, Tidal), metadata, ISRC codes, copyright registration, 30-day monitoring. Duration: Days 20–21+.
