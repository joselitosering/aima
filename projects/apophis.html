02 — Production Logistics

How We Built It

Every production decision documented — from brief to final delivery.

🎯
Phase 01
Discovery & Brief
Client intake, creative brief, objective mapping, audience profiling, platform strategy. Duration: Days 1–2.
📋
Phase 02
Pre-Production
Script, storyboard, shot list, location and talent coordination, technical spec lock. Duration: Days 3–5.
🎬
Phase 03
Production
Principal photography, drone, b-roll, voiceover, and AI-assisted on-set direction. Duration: Days 6–8.
✂️
Phase 04
Post-Production
Offline edit, color grade, sound design, AI VFX, motion graphics, captions. Duration: Days 9–12.
🔍
Phase 05
Review & Revisions
Two structured revision rounds, timestamped feedback, client portal sign-off. Duration: Days 13–14.
🚀
Phase 06
Delivery & Publish
Platform exports, upload, metadata, ad account push, 30-day monitoring. Duration: Day 14+.
